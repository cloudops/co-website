Cloud Solutions for Healthcare

By providing consulting and managed services, we empower medical professionals with the means to deliver innovation and patient care at scale.

The healthcare industry is in the midst of a major transformation, moving towards a digital delivery model. CloudOps is a trusted, solutions-focused advisor with significant experience enabling researchers, institutions, and healthcare communities to collaborate using secure cloud platform solutions.

Healthcare is ready to succeed in the cloud

Scalability and security

Cloud solutions enable institutions to scale their resource consumption on demand - without compromising on security.

Big data and analytics

The cloud provides an Ideal platform for storing and federating big data that can be analyzed by data science and machine learning approaches to turn data into information into insights, enabling medical research to realize the promise of evidence based, precision medicine.

Telemedicine

Patients are looking for the flexibility to replace in-person patient visits with remote options whenever possible. Cloud platforms support auto-scaling of resources to support connecting users from wherever they may be, to the help they need wherever it happens to be.

Interoperability

With appropriately informed consent, policy based access control and immutable records, data stored in standards-based formats in the cloud can be accessed by authorized stakeholders, ensuring software defined governance, and allowing trusted cooperation between various segments of the healthcare industry, including hospitals, clinics, pharmaceuticals, insurance, and payment.

Things to consider

Operational excellence
  • The cloud is not a destination but a journey of continuous improvement
  • Operational excellence drives outcomes
  • Optimize CapEx vs OpEx models of adoption
Software as a Medical Device
  • Regulators have converged on the SaMD standard
  • Software dealing with clinical data must be treated through a similar compliance framework as are medical devices.
Security
  • Consent based confidentiality of patient information combine with industry regulations
  • SOC2 Type 2 is a foundational standard underlying many related compliance requirements and standards
  • Increasingly sophisticated cyberattacks
