                    <h2>Software pipelines, middleware and infrastructure for <b>Cloud-based Software Companies</b></h2>                  
                    <p>Cloud-based Software Companies usually accumulate large amounts of data, which makes them particularly attractive targets for hackers. Entrusted to protect personal data, fCloud-based Software Companies have a responsibility to address security issues and safeguard information in order to minimize the risk of breaches. </p>
                    <p>While Cloud-based Software Companies try to prevent data breaches, their ecosystem is constantly changing. There are multiple factors that make this task even more difficult.</p>
                    <p>In this white paper we will highlight how to secure your DevOps platform, especially for those operating under the enterprise umbrella.</p>
