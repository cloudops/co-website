Featured Role

Director of Managed Services

Are you a hands-on people leader capable of growing and driving team success through coaching and mentoring? You'll fit right in. Keep Reading.

As the Director of Managed Services, you will be responsible for the day-to-day leadership of a talented team of cloud operators and specialists who oversee our customer environments across CloudOps’ managed services. In this role, areas of responsibility include identifying inefficiencies, challenges and opportunities in our “People, Process, Products and Practice” and providing a business-level layer of customer service and escalation.

This position can be located anywhere in Canada as the team works primarily remotely and serves customers nationwide.
