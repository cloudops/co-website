Cloud Migration

Migrating workloads to the cloud doesn’t have to be overwhelming. CloudOps will partner with you to plan your move step-by-step, execute it with care, and set you up for operational success.

You're ready to move to the cloud

You understand the business advantages that come with a cloud operational model. The wider market does too. In fact, this year some 83% of enterprise workloads will already be in the cloud.

Why cloud?

Limit spiraling costs

Gone is the need to predict resource requirements 6, 12, or 36 months down the line. Scale with business activity while stabilizing infrastructure costs.

Increase agility

The cloud frees you to try new things quickly, take small steps, and iterate. Increase speed to market by taking full advantage of cloud infrastructure and automation.

Transform legacy infrastructure

Become a digital organization. Migrate existing applications to the cloud using right-sized and interoperable components to minimize waste.

The challenge of getting it right the first time

The benefits of using the cloud are clear. Yet most cloud projects fail to meet expectations. You’re looking for a trusted partner who can help you navigate the challenges that come with migrating workloads to the cloud.

Lack of expertise

You haven’t done this before and lack the skills, knowledge, or culture required

High stakes

You have deadlines to hit for digital transformation and need to get this right

Security concerns

Cloud security can be a barrier to adoption and a severe liability if done wrong

Which cloud?

AWS, GCP, Azure, regional, edge, private, hybrid, and multi-cloud. Which do you choose?

Which applications?

You have many workloads to move with applications that may or may not be cloud ready

Many requirements

You need to hit governance, resilience, performance, cost, and operational requirements

You need a trusted partner

Over 15 years of learning and solving cloud problems, CloudOps has built the expertise needed to guide you in which workloads should and shouldn’t be moved into the cloud.

The sad truth is that most lift and shift projects are likely to fail. Without a plan to refactor and re-platform applications to take advantage of the cloud, you’re headed for ballooning costs and stability issues—the opposite of what you’ve signed up for.

Our cloud migration team makes it easy for you to succeed. We look for patterns in your application and architecture that can easily be made cloud native. Not only do we get you in the cloud, we set you up for long-term success with our workshops, knowledge transfer, and ongoing support. CloudOps prides itself on being cloud agnostic but opinionated. We will always steer you towards solutions that maximize your freedom, flexibility, and long-term operational success, allowing you to own your destiny in the cloud™.

Working with us, you will have a trusted partner on your side who sets you up for long-term success in the cloud. We will help you succeed not just in your first deployment but in the long game, enabling you to iterate and evolve your application platform over time.
