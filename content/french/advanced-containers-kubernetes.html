Conteneurs et Kubernetes - Niveau avancé

Au cours de cet atelier, vous apprendrez:

  • Day to day operations and best practices around Kubernetes;
  • Concepts around HA, securing, logging and monitoring of Kubernetes.

This workshop is available on Azure, GCP and AWS.

Durée: 2 jours

$3000/participant (minimum 3 participants)

Conditions préalables:
  • Experience using Linux CLI and a general understanding of virtualization and container technology;
  • Good understanding of Docker and Kubernetes concepts and features.
Exigences techniques:

Mac, Linux OS or Windows laptop with SSH client (putty, cygwin), web browser supporting HTML5

Plan de cours

Jour 1
  • Advanced Scheduling
  • RBAC
  • Security
  • Advanced Networking
  • Auto-scaling
  • Federation
  • Advanced Helm
  • Third Party Resources
  • Kubernetes Operators
  • Rkt as an alternative to Docker and CRI
Jour 2
  • Cloud Provider Configurations
  • Securing etcd
  • Add-ons
  • Cluster High Availability
  • Upgrades
  • Logging Best Practices
  • Monitoring and Alerting Best Practices
  • Deployment Best Practices
  • Microservices Best Practices
